**Voltage Control Optimization using Pyomo for Distribution Systems**  
*Independent Research Project*  

- Developed a Pyomo-based optimization model for voltage control in radial distribution networks using a linearized power-flow approximation.
- Formulated reactive power control decisions at selected buses to reduce voltage-limit violations under varying load scenarios.
- Compared voltage profiles before and after optimization and evaluated violation reduction, minimum voltage improvement, and control effort.
- Tested the optimization pipeline on a main feeder and an unseen feeder topology to assess transferability across distribution-network structures.
- Tools: Python, Pyomo, Highs, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, YAML, Git.
